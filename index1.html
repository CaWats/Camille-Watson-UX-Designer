About the project

Snapshot

The (Federal) Department of Education is the peak body for national curriculum in Australia. The site caters for many different users and the original design was inconsistent due to the many needs of its users.

The Prototype

  • The current site
  • User testing results

The problem

A messy and complicated information architecture meant that complex menus were hard to navigate for users.
The Focal Points

It is crucial to remember that the Department of Education doesnt only cater for public servants but also parents, guardians and other caregivers.

The plans for the redesign were to address the architecture of the site and provide a better navigation for users to locate information easily across all stages of the education journey.
